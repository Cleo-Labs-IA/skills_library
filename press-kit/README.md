## What is this press kit?

This folder contains the official, ready-to-publish assets and language for `skills_library` — the world's first open-source compliance skills library for AI coding agents (40 skills, MIT-licensed, released May 28, 2026).

Everything in this folder is **public domain for editorial use**. Quote it, lift it, remix it. You don't need to ask. If you do anything cool with it, drop us a line.

## How to use it

**Journalists & bloggers** — Start with `one-liners.md` for your headline, grab a `quotes.md` pull-quote, copy the `company-bio.md` short version into your boilerplate.

**Podcast hosts** — `founder-bio.md` (medium) is your intro script. `quotes.md` gives you discussion seeds. `key-facts.md` is your prep sheet.

**Partners & integrators** — `messaging.md` tells you how we describe what we do. `company-bio.md` gives you co-marketing copy.

**PR teams** — `announcement-press-release.md` is your ready-to-distribute template. Swap in your outlet's house style and send.

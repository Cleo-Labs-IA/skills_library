## What we respond to fastest

In order of expected turnaround:

1. **Press release / fact-check requests** — under 4 hours during Paris business hours
2. **Interview / podcast booking requests** — under 24 hours, includes proposed time slots
3. **Demo requests (Cleo Legal API)** — under 24 hours, includes calendar link
4. **Asset requests (logos, screenshots, video)** — under 48 hours
5. **Partnership / co-marketing requests** — within 1 week (longer process, intentional)

---

## What to include in your email

To get the fastest, highest-quality response, please include:

- **Your outlet / show name and audience size**
- **Your angle or working headline** (helps us route to the right tailored quote)
- **Your deadline** (we will respect it; help us prioritize)
- **Format** (written, audio, video, live)
- **Any specific assets you need** (logos, screenshots, founder photo, video)

A four-line email gets a faster, better answer than a six-paragraph one.

---

## What we won't do

Sorry in advance for the following — these are firm:

- **"Quick exclusive" embargo asks without a confirmed publish date.** Embargoes only with a signed date.
- **Sponsored content disguised as editorial.** Happy to advertise — but it gets labeled as such.
- **Off-the-record claims about competitors.** We'll stay on-record about our own product instead.
- **Legal advice quotes.** We are not attorneys. We don't pretend to be.

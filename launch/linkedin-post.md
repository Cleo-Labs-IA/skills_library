# LinkedIn Post — Launch

We just open-sourced our compliance skills library.

After building regulatory AI at Cleo Labs, we packaged our compliance knowledge into 28 reusable AI skills for Claude Code.

They work with Claude Code (Anthropic's CLI). You install them in 30 seconds:

git clone https://github.com/Cleo-Labs-IA/skills_library.git ~/.claude/skills/comply

Then you just ask:
"I'm launching a face cream with retinol in the EU and US. What do I need?"

And Claude gives you:
→ Substance check against 13 databases (CosIng, REACH, FDA, Prop 65...)
→ Country-specific labeling requirements (INCI, allergens, responsible person)
→ Market entry checklist (CPNP notification, FDA registration, timeline, cost)
→ Customs classification and landed cost calculation

No regulatory consultant. No 6-week assessment. Instant, actionable compliance guidance.

Who is this for?
• Small physical product companies (1-10 people)
• Cosmetics, food, supplements, electronics, toys, textiles
• Anyone selling across EU, US, UK, Japan, Korea, Canada

What's inside:
• product-compliance — substance check against 13 regulatory databases
• labeling-compliance — country-specific labeling rules
• market-entry-checklist — step-by-step market entry
• customs-and-trade — HS codes, duties, landed cost, sanctions
• regulatory-intelligence — monitor signals that could block your product
• multi-jurisdiction-scan — parallel scan across all target markets
• + 6 more skills for remediation, reporting, evidence, frameworks

It's free. MIT license. PRs welcome.

This is the compliance toolkit we wish existed when we started.

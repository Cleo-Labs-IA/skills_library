Product Core
product-compliance
Check ingredients against 13 substance databases. Verdict per substance per jurisdiction.
labeling-compliance
Country-specific labeling rules: INCI, allergens, warnings, responsible person, CE/UKCA.
market-entry-checklist
Step-by-step checklist to enter a new market. Classify, regulate, test, label, certify, ship.
substance-screening
Deep ingredient screening: INCI to CAS to 13 databases to verdict with margin calculation.
Market Intelligence
regulatory-intelligence
Monitor regulatory signals that could block your product from sale. Bans, recalls, deadlines.
multi-jurisdiction-scan
Parallel scan across all target markets. One agent per jurisdiction, color-coded verdicts.
customs-and-trade
HS classification, duty rates, landed cost, dual-use screening, sanctions check.
regulatory-calendar
Track every compliance deadline: enforcement dates, certificate expiry, renewal schedules.
Action
compliance-audit-sprint
Pre-launch compliance sprint. Category to market-ready in one session.
compliance-remediation
Fix compliance blockers: reformulate, relabel, certify. Actionable steps, not theory.
evidence-blitz
Gather all compliance evidence for certification or audit in parallel. Docs, proofs, certs.
recall-response
Product recall workflow from trigger to close-out. Notification deadlines per market.
Deep Dives
packaging-compliance
EPR registration, PPWR transition, recycling symbols, SUP restrictions, plastic tax.
testing-certification
Map required tests per product per market. Lab selection, costs, timelines, renewals.
responsible-person
Set up your EU RP, UK RP, US Agent, EAEU AR. Mandate letters, costs, obligations.
claims-substantiation
Validate marketing claims against EU 655/2013, FDA, FTC, Green Claims Directive.
import-export-docs
Generate trade documents: invoices, certificates of origin, DG declarations, Incoterms.
marketplace-compliance
Amazon, Shopify, Walmart, Etsy, TikTok Shop compliance requirements.
Verticals
cosmetics-compliance
Full cosmetics compliance across 8 markets: EU, US, UK, Japan, Korea, China, Canada, ASEAN.
food-compliance
Food and beverage: General Food Law, FSMA, allergen declarations, novel foods, HACCP.
electronics-compliance
CE, FCC, UKCA, RoHS, WEEE, battery regulation, Cyber Resilience Act.
textile-compliance
REACH for textiles, PFAS, fiber labeling, flammability, OEKO-TEX, Digital Product Passport.
toy-compliance
EN 71 suite, CPSIA, ASTM F963, China CCC, age grading, chemical migration, phthalates.
sustainability-compliance
CSRD, CSDDD, EUDR, CBAM, EPR per country, ESPR, Green Claims Directive.
product-safety-incident
Safety incident from detection to closure. CPSC 24h, EU Safety Gate 10 days, risk matrix.
Reference
compliance-frameworks-ref
Product regulation reference: EU 1223/2009, REACH, MoCRA, Prop 65, GPSR, EN 71, RoHS.

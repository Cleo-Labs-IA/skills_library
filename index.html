See it in action

Real prompts, real answers

claude-code
$I'm launching a face cream with retinol in the EU and US. What do I need?
  • ✓ Retinol: restricted to 0.3% body / 0.05% face (EU Annex III, 2025 limits)
  • ✓ EU: CPNP notification + EU Responsible Person + PIF + CPSR
  • ✓ US: FDA MoCRA registration + facility listing + GMP by Dec 2026
  • ✓ Labeling: INCI list, 26 EU allergens, batch code, PAO symbol
  • ✓ Timeline: ~8 weeks EU, ~4 weeks US
claude-code
$What certifications for a Bluetooth speaker in US and EU?
  • ✓ EU: CE marking (RED 2014/53/EU + LVD + EMC), RoHS, WEEE registration
  • ✓ US: FCC Part 15 Subpart C, UL listing (Amazon requires it)
  • ✓ Battery: UN 38.3 transport test, EU Battery Regulation 2023/1542
  • ! Cyber: EU Cyber Resilience Act (2027 deadline)
  • ✓ Cost: €8-15K testing, 6-12 weeks
claude-code
$I want to export my vitamin supplements to Japan
  • ✓ Food Sanitation Act: import notification to quarantine station
  • ! Health claims: only permitted claims (FOSHU or Foods with Function Claims)
  • ✓ Labeling: Japanese language required, 8 mandatory allergens
  • ✓ HS code: 2106.90 (food preparations), duty 8.8%
  • ✓ Landed cost: product + 8.8% duty + 10% consumption tax

# Real-World Examples

## 🧴 Launching a face cream in the EU

**You:** "I'm launching a face cream with retinol, niacinamide, and hyaluronic acid in France, Germany, and the UK. What do I need?"

**Comply triggers:** `product-compliance` → `labeling-compliance` → `market-entry-checklist`

**Result:**
- Retinol: restricted under EU Cosmetics Regulation Annex III — max 0.3% in body products, 0.05% in face (new 2025 limits)
- EU: CPNP notification required, Responsible Person in EU, INCI labeling, 26 allergen check, PIF (Product Information File)
- UK: Separate SCPN notification, UK Responsible Person, UKCA compliance
- Timeline: ~6-8 weeks for EU, +2 weeks for UK

## 🔌 Selling an electronic device in the US and EU

**You:** "I have a Bluetooth speaker I want to sell in the US and EU. What certifications do I need?"

**Comply triggers:** `compliance-audit-sprint` → `compliance-frameworks-ref`

**Result:**
- EU: CE marking (RED 2014/53/EU for radio), RoHS, WEEE registration, GPSR responsible person, DoC
- US: FCC Part 15 certification, UL listing (voluntary but Amazon requires), California Prop 65 warning
- Timeline: FCC ~4-6 weeks, CE ~3-4 weeks (if tested in parallel)
- Cost estimate: $3K-8K for testing + certification

## 🍫 Exporting food products to Japan

**You:** "I make artisanal chocolate bars and want to export to Japan. What's the process?"

**Comply triggers:** `market-entry-checklist` → `customs-and-trade` → `labeling-compliance`

**Result:**
- Food Sanitation Act: import notification to quarantine station
- JAS (Japanese Agricultural Standards): voluntary but builds trust
- Labeling: Japanese language required, allergen declarations (7 mandatory + 21 recommended), nutritional facts, importer name/address
- HS code: 1806.31 (filled chocolate blocks), duty rate: 10%
- Landed cost breakdown: product + 10% duty + 10% consumption tax + handling

## 🧸 Checking toy safety for EU market

**You:** "Is my plush toy with button eyes safe to sell in the EU for children under 3?"

**Comply triggers:** `product-compliance` → `compliance-remediation`

**Result:**
- EN 71-1 (mechanical/physical): button eyes = choking hazard for <36 months → FAIL
- EN 71-2 (flammability): textile material needs flame test
- EN 71-3 (migration of elements): dye must pass heavy metals test
- REACH Annex XVII: phthalate restrictions on soft PVC components
- Remediation: replace button eyes with embroidered eyes for 0-3 age group, commission EN 71 test suite (~€800-1500)

## 📊 Preparing for Amazon EU marketplace

**You:** "I need compliance docs for my skincare line to list on Amazon EU"

**Comply triggers:** `evidence-blitz` → `compliance-reporting`

**Result: 4 parallel agents gather:**
1. Safety docs: CPSR (Cosmetic Product Safety Report), stability tests, challenge tests
2. Labeling: INCI list accuracy check, claims substantiation, allergen declarations
3. Substance docs: CoA (Certificate of Analysis) per ingredient, SDS for restricted substances
4. Market certs: CPNP notification confirmation, Responsible Person details, DoC

Export: compliance matrix (product × market × requirement × status) ready for Amazon Seller Central

12-step roadmap:
1. Classify: food supplement (NemV) vs medicine (AMG) — borderline
2. Check VitD upper limit (BfR): 20 µg/day
3. Health claims must be EU Reg 432/2012 approved
4. Notification: Bundesamt für Verbraucherschutz (BVL) — fee €0
5. Label: German language mandatory
6. Allergens, batch, MHD, importer address
7. EPR Lucid registration (LUCID-Nr.)
8. VAT registration if >€10k/yr B2C
9. PFAND if bottled water/drink
10-12. Customs HS 2106.90.92, distributor partner.
